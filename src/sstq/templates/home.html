Why this project exists

Consumers increasingly care about where products come from, but most product pages still hide the sourcing, assembly, transport, and retail story behind a wall of vague claims.

SSTQ gives you a clearer route: start with a barcode, inspect the timeline, review claim evidence, and understand how different countries contributed to the finished item.
